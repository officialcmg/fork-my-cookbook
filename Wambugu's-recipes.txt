Recipe: Garlic Shrimp Butter Pasta
By: example-student

Ingredients:
- 200g pasta
- 2 tbsp butter
- 3 cloves garlic
- Salt
- shrimp
- tomatoes

Steps:
1. Boil pasta until tender.
2. Melt butter, add chopped garlic, cook 1 min.
3. Toss pasta in the garlic butter. Salt to taste.
4. Boil the shrimp
5. Add tomatoes to the shrimp 
6. Combine with the pasta
